Trouble Orders

Trouble Orders cannot be imported fully because one of the following problems:

  • SKU: The UPC or SKU is unknown to Capacity
  • Price: The price does not match the authorized price for this retailer
  • Cust: The retailer's store is unknown to Capacity or there is an error in the customer address
  • Dupe: This order number has been received previously
  • Ship: The shipping method is unrecognized or not configured

Contact help@capacityllc.com for assistance in resolving these import problems.
